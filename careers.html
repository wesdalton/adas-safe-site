Opportunity

As we rapidly expand throughout the East Coast with our dedication to OEM adherence, career growth opportunities at ADAS Safe are nearly endless.


No matter your level of expertise, we'll find a place for you. And once you get here, you'll get all the training you need to succeed.

How it works

Experienced

+

You know the tools, the technology, and how to get the job done right — wherever the vehicle is. You can diagnose, program, and calibrate across a wide range of makes and models. You’re the one other techs call when they’re stuck, and you’re always ready to lend a hand. If you're looking for variety, autonomy, and a fast-paced environment, our Mobile Technician role might be the perfect fit.

Speciality Opportunities

+

We’re always looking for standout individuals with specialized skills or leadership potential. Whether you're interested in taking on management-level responsibilities, mentoring newer technicians, or stepping into a high-impact role, we offer unique opportunities beyond the typical technician path. If you're looking for more than just a job — and ready to grow with us — we want to hear from you.

Dedicated To Your Development

We don’t just help you get by — we help you get ahead. As a Mobile Technician, you’ll receive tiered training that starts with ADAS calibration foundations and progresses to advanced diagnostics and electrical work. You’ll learn from the best, use cutting-edge technology, solve real-world problems in the field, and have a full support system behind you. Whether you're experienced or looking to level up, we’ll give you the tools and mentorship to grow.
