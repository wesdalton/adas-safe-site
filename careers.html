Join Us

The ADAS industry is the fastest growing industry in the automotive sector, yet we are growing faster. Whether you're a beginner technician or experienced, we have something for everyone. Every day, we work together to refine, develop, and explore ways to enhance the safety of our roads through a commitment to performing ADAS calibrations and diagnostics the right way. Join us in shaping the world of automotive safety.

Opportunity

As we rapidly expanded in Florida with our dedication to OEM adherence, career growth opportunities at ADAS Safe are nearly endless.


No matter your level of expertise, we'll find a place for you. And once you get here, you'll get all the training you need to succeed.

How it works

Some Experience

+

Do you have some ADAS calibration and diagnostic experience under your belt? You may be at that point where you understand the more you get your hands dirty, the more you learn. The ADAS space is changing – and the work gets more and more interesting. If this sounds fun, check out ADAS Safe's Junior or Associate technician positions.

Experienced

+

You've been there; you've done that. You've used all the tools of the trade. You can diagnose and calibrate just about anything. You even like coaching up some of the newer technicians in the industry when they have questions. If you want to work on many different makes and models, you might be a perfect fit for our Senior Technician role. And your career doesn't stop there. If you want to develop and are interested in becoming a Manager, we have that, too.

Speciality Opportunities

+

We've got other opportunities that often require specialized skills or training, like wheel alignment specialists and our mobile tech team, which performs on-site calibrations and helps customers at their locations or out on the road.

Dedicated To Your Development

We don't just help you get by; we help you get ahead. We offer many tiers of training, from ADAS calibration foundations to advanced courses in electrical diagnostics. By completing our custom training courses, you can start as an Intake Specialist and be on your way to becoming a Senior Technician. You'll learn from the best, have access to the latest technology, solve unique problems daily, and have an army of technicians here to support you.

Our People

We firmly believe that our company's success depends on its people. We're proud and lucky to have a team that loves to work together, shares insights and support, and celebrates each other's victories. We invest in a happy and healthy workforce and an internal learning and development program that covers every phase of your career.
